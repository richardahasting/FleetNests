{{ club.name if club else 'Club' }} Member Rules
Safety is no accident. Every member is responsible for knowing and following these rules on every trip.
  1. Generally, The Enclave residents & CLA Pilots only.
  2. Inspect the boat & prop before and after each trip.
  3. Fill with gas when finished or leave cash in the cash bottle.
  4. Follow all of the Captain's Checklist every time. View Checklist
  5. Every trip must have a designated Captain in charge who is responsible and liable for everything and everyone during the voyage.
  6. Motor must always be off when swimmers are in the water.
  7. Initial fee is non-refundable unless you find a suitable replacement member to take your place in the Club.
  8. Monthly payments due on the 1st — pay by Zelle, Venmo, or PayPal to Steve Cossette. Members share equally in all routine maintenance & repairs.
  9. The Member is always responsible for the boat, fuel, and damages during use by them or any family member.
  10. Only Members may reserve and operate the boat. Non-members such as family require training and approval.
  11. Any boat issues or emergencies while on the lake: Call Marina at 830-255-7442. Tell them you are a member — they will help or send someone out pronto. Marina membership is in the name of Steve Cossette.
  12. Steve Cossette has final say on any issues or disputes.
